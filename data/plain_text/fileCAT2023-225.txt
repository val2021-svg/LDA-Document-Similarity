Syllabus  Those weekly courses are offered at several levels, depending on the  results of the placement test. Classes are organized as practical workshops  focusing on oral understanding and communication, written understanding  and communication, structural proficiency (grammar, vocabulary).  Students will work individually or in groups on themes related to  contemporary French culture in relation to its historical past.
